# West Texas Association for Botany
Our general goals are: 
1) build a positive and active plant research environment; 
2) open a door for undergraduate students and the public to peek into the leading researches. 

Outline of events:

1. Academic Events (every other month): sharing your academic milestones, such as research progresses, grant/fellowship submission and professional presentation. Let your peers cheer for you!

2. Professional Development Events (once per semester): invite senior researchers to share their career paths and insights into academic conflicts.
